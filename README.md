#FOOD WASTAGE MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

#Problem Statement 
Now-a Days large amount of foods are being wasted in many hotels, events etc. So the project aims in  2 things about food waste management including , the distribution of the surplus foods to the needy and to dispose the expired food in an eco-friedly way .

#Objectives
*Track surplus foods
*Donate the food to the needy people
*Identify the expired food
*Reduce food waste

#Tech Stack
-Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
-Backend:Node.js,Express
-Database:MongoDB
